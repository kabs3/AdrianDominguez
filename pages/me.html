Who i am?


My name is Adrián, I was born and raised in Mexico City, I am the youngest of five brothers.
I am a sociable person, I like to work with a good team. I am persevering in my work and school
I like everything that has to do with technology and new trends. Above all get knowledge and apply it but never stop learning.
When I finished high school I did not continue with my education and I got to work for three years as a data capturist in Cosmocolor S.A. de C.V., work in which I managed to perform until I got the post of person in charge of a credentials module. However, I realized that I wanted something more than that job and that's when I decided to resume my studies in 2014, entering the engineering faculty of the UNAM in the mining and metallurgical engineering career, but I did not like this career. I did an admission exam for the Computer Engineering course. During my stay in the faculty, I have worked as a teacher in mathematics and physics to be able to pay some expenses.
